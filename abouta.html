How did I get into Product Design

I've always had a creative vision, especially for fashion, so I knew I wanted to study something related to it while growing up. Still, like every third Indian, I got into engineering. I started looking for something that blended my desire to flourish without any artistic boundaries yet stay within the technology industry.
Since my freshman year, I started participating in VOGUE, a fashion show organized at our cultural fests and beauty pageants. From there, I explored fashion designing and, eventually, graphic designing. I started reading and learning about design practices and learned about the term Product Design. I always tended to help people and solve problems and involving the user through every stage of the Design process made me more deeply connected to this. I started understanding design and finally realized where I am, What I want to do and where I want to be.
